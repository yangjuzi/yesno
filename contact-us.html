Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the readings on YesNo.Pro? +

Our divination tools are designed as guidance instruments, not fortune-telling devices. The accuracy depends on your openness to the wisdom offered and how you apply it to your situation. We encourage using our tools as a starting point for reflection and personal insight.

Is my personal information and questions kept private? +

Absolutely. We take privacy very seriously. Your questions, readings, and personal information are kept completely confidential. We do not store your questions or share any personal data with third parties. Please see our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Can I get personalized readings or consultations? +

Currently, YesNo.Pro offers automated guidance tools. However, we're working on introducing personalized consultation services. Contact us at guidance@yesno.pro to express your interest and we'll notify you when these services become available.

How often can I use the divination tools? +

You can use our tools as often as you like. However, for the best experience, we recommend reflecting on each reading before seeking another. Sometimes the universe needs time to reveal its wisdom, and too many consecutive readings might cloud the message.

Do you offer mobile apps? +

Currently, YesNo.Pro is available as a responsive web application that works beautifully on all devices. We're considering developing dedicated mobile apps in the future. Follow our blog or contact us to stay updated on new developments.
